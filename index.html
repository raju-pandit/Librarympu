About

To cater to the talented and skilled human resource needed for the global technological and socio-economic advancement MADHYANCHAL PROFESSIONAL UNIVERSITY (MPU) was established in 2018 under the aegis of Vanshpati Smriti Shiksha Samiti (VSSS), Bhopal. Since 2002, the Samiti has been promoting a group of 8 colleges at Bhopal and Indore under the banner of PATEL GROUP OF INSTITUTIONS (PGOI) in professional fields ranging from Diploma to Post graduation imparting knowledge and skills to think ‘Beyond the Future’. In the span of 18 years PGOI has earned its name and fame not only in the country but also abroad; to expand higher education for socio-economic development of the Nation besides scientific and technological advancement.
